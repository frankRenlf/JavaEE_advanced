CITY & LEEDS

Leeds, the third largest city in the UK, is the capital of West Yorkshire, England

Environment

Leeds is one of the four cities awarded the title of "good environment city" by the British government.It is also one of the best green cities in Europe.

Economy

Leeds is the second largest financial center and the second largest legal center in the UK. It is an important economic, commercial, industrial, cultural and transportation hub in Central England. It is one of the eight core cities in England.

Transport

It has a well-developed railway and road network connecting southern England and Scotland. There is a canal connecting Liverpool to the Irish Sea in the West. Leeds Bradford international is built three kilometers northwest of the city airport.
